# Items, Weapons, and Magic Reference

## 1. Rarity System
Items follow a standard color-coded rarity system:
- **Common (White):** Standard gear, basic materials.
- **Uncommon (Green):** Minor enchantments.
- **Rare (Blue):** Significant stat boosts, unique properties.
- **Epic (Purple):** Artifacts crafted by masters, strong magical auras.
- **Legendary (Gold):** Items of myth, capable of changing the landscape.
- **Mythic (Red):** One-of-a-kind items tied to the lore/Calamities.

## 2. Tools & Gathering
Players have access to a variety of survival and gathering tools:
- **Pickaxes, Axes, and Sickles:** Used for mining ore, chopping wood, and gathering herbs respectively.
- **Crafting Kits:** Portable tools for field repairs and basic alchemy.
- *Note:* Like standard weapons, basic tools degrade with use and will eventually break.

## 3. Weapons & Durability
- **Standard Weapons:** (Swords, Shields, Bows, Staves). Normal weapons found or crafted by the player suffer from **Durability Loss**. As they are used in combat, they become rusty and chipped, dealing less damage. Eventually, they will break completely if not repaired.
- **Awakening Weapons (Custom/Named Weapons):** Extremely rare weapons (like the demonic sword *BloodHound*). These do not break. Instead, they bond with the player and **Rank Up** alongside them, gaining new stats and unique skills through combat experience.

## 4. Magic System (Spells)
Magic is divided by Elements and Tiers (1 to 10).
- **Fire Magic:** Focuses on AoE and damage over time (Burns). Ex: `Tier 1: Ignite`, `Tier 5: Hellfire`.
- **Water/Ice Magic:** Crowd control and healing. Ex: `Tier 2: Frost Nova`, `Tier 6: Absolute Zero`.
- **Light/Holy Magic:** Healing, buffing, and high damage against Undead. Ex: `Tier 3: Holy Ray`, `Tier 7: Resurrection`.
- **Dark/Necromancy:** Debuffs, summoning minions. Ex: `Tier 4: Life Drain`, `Tier 8: Army of the Dead`.

## 5. Consumables & Materials
- **Health/Mana Potions (Minor to Supreme):** Restores HP/MP.
- **Monster Cores:** Dropped by monsters C-Rank and above. Used for crafting magical weapons or absorbing for EXP.
- **Elixirs of Awakening:** Rare potions that artificially increase a player's Rank without an Awakening Quest.
