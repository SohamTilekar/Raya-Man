# Mobs Reference Manual

## Overview
Mobs (Monsters and Entities) are categorized by their hostility and their **Rank** (F to SSS). The AI Director determines which mobs spawn based on the player's current Rank and the time spent in the current area.

---

## 1. Peaceful Mobs
Peaceful mobs will never attack the player, even if provoked. They usually flee when attacked.

| Mob Name | Rank | Biome | Behavior / Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mana Rabbit** | F | Grasslands, Forests | Flee on sight. Drops: Small Meat, Mana Crystal Shard. |
| **Crystal Stag** | E | Deep Forest, Mountains | Roams gracefully. Will run fast if approached. Drops: Crystal Horn, Quality Meat. |
| **Sunlight Butterfly** | F | Meadows, Elven Woods | Flocks around magical flora. Drops: Luminous Dust. |
| **Woolly Ox** | E | Plains, Tundra | Travels in herds. Drops: Heavy Leather, Ox Horn. |

---

## 2. Neutral & Trap Mobs
Neutral mobs mind their own business but will fight back fiercely if attacked or if the player intrudes on their territory. Trap mobs disguise themselves.

| Mob Name | Rank | Biome | Behavior / Drops |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Iron Tusk Boar** | E | Forests, Foothills | Forages for food. Charges if the player gets too close. Drops: Boar Tusk, Coarse Leather. |
| **Stone Golem** | D | Mountains, Caves | Dormant until struck. High defense (Tank). Drops: Stone Core, Iron Ore. |
| **Treant (Elder Wood)** | C | Deep Forests | Disguised as trees. Awakens if trees are chopped nearby. Drops: Enchanted Wood, Sap. |
| **Mimic Chest** | C | Dungeons, Ruins | Disguised as a loot chest to punish greedy players. Deals massive ambush damage. Drops: Rare Loot, Mimic Core. |
| **Wild Gryphon** | B | High Peaks | Hunts smaller prey. Will dive-bomb if its nest is approached. Drops: Gryphon Feather, Beast Core. |

---

## 3. Hostile Mobs
Hostile mobs actively hunt the player. Their spawns are heavily controlled by the AI Director to match or exceed the player's Danger Level.

### Low-Tier (F - D Rank)
- **Slime (F-Rank):** Weak, slow, physical resistance. Often the first enemy spawned by the AI to test the player.
- **Goblin Scavenger (F-Rank):** Hunts in packs, uses primitive weapons.
- **Dire Wolf (E-Rank):** Fast, flanks the player, attacks in packs.
- **Werewolf (D-Rank):** Spawns at night. High regeneration, fast physical attacks.

### Mid-Tier (C - B Rank)
- **Orc Berserker (C-Rank):** High health and damage. Acts as a frontline tank for other monsters.
- **Shadow Assassin (C-Rank):** Uses stealth and backstabs. Spawns if the player stays in one place for too long.
- **Wyvern (B-Rank):** Lesser dragonkin. Breathes fire/poison, can fly and grab the player.

### High-Tier / Calamities (A - SSS Rank)
*These are spawned as major AI Events to push the narrative.*
- **Demon General (A-Rank):** Commands lesser mobs. Casts high-tier dark magic. 
- **Shadow Clone (A-Rank):** A terrifying opponent spawned by the AI that perfectly copies the player's class, stats, and current weapons.
- **Ancient Lich (S-Rank):** Summons endless undead hordes. Usually found deep in corrupted biomes.
- **Abyssal Dragon (SS-Rank):** A realm-ending threat. Massive AoE damage, near-impenetrable scales.
- **The Calamity (SSS-Rank):** Unique entities generated by the AI to force a world-altering event.
