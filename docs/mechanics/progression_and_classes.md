# Progression, Classes, and Ranks

## 1. The Rank System
In this world, power is absolute and is measured in Ranks. Both players and NPCs are bound by this system.
- **F-Rank:** Normal humans, beginners. Only capable of fighting weak slimes or mana rabbits.
- **E-Rank:** Novice Adventurers. Can use basic magic or weapon arts.
- **D-Rank:** Experienced Fighters. Capable of taking down minor threats.
- **C-Rank:** Elite Soldiers, Knights.
- **B-Rank:** Master Class. Often serve as Guild Masters or Royal Guards.
- **A-Rank:** National Treasures. Their power can turn the tide of wars.
- **S-Rank:** Transcendent. One-man armies capable of destroying cities.
- **SS-Rank:** Demigods.
- **SSS-Rank:** Mythological figures, capable of altering the fabric of the world.

*Note: The AI tracks the player's Rank against the world Danger Level. Stagnating at a low rank for too long will lead to overwhelming enemy encounters.*

## 2. Class & Combat System
Combat utilizes a classic party-based Role system (Tank, DPS, Support), often seen in manhwa. Even if the player is solo, they must choose a path or rely on summoned/NPC companions.

### The Tank System
- **Role:** Draws aggro (attention) from enemies, absorbs massive damage.
- **Skills:** `Taunt`, `Shield Wall`, `Indomitable Will`.
- **Mechanic:** Mobs controlled by the AI will naturally target the entity outputting the most damage or healing. Tanks must use specific skills to force the AI to target them instead. 

### DPS (Damage Dealer)
- **Melee DPS (Assassin/Berserker):** High burst damage, lower defense. Skills: `Backstab`, `Blood Rage`.
- **Ranged/Magic DPS (Mage/Archer):** High area-of-effect damage, extremely fragile. Skills: `Fireball`, `Piercing Shot`.

### Support (Healer/Buffer)
- **Role:** Keeps the party alive, buffs stats.
- **Skills:** `Holy Light`, `Mana Barrier`, `Haste`.

## 3. Leveling Up
- **Experience Points (EXP):** Gained by killing monsters, completing AI-generated events, or exploring new biomes.
- **Awakening:** To jump from one Rank to the next (e.g., C to B), the player must complete a specific "Awakening Quest" tailored by the AI Director based on their class and past choices.
